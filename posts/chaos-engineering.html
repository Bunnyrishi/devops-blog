Chaos engineering is the discipline of experimenting on distributed systems to build confidence in system resilience and discover weaknesses before they cause outages.

"Chaos engineering is not about breaking things, it's about learning how things break." - Netflix

  • Observability: Comprehensive monitoring during tests
  • Automation: Continuous chaos testing
